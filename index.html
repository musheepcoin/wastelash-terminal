The Trading Outpost

The Outpost is a waypoint in the middle of the wasteland, a trading counter where recovered things pile up. Fragments, images, memories torn from ruins or scavenged on black markets. Nothing here is complete, nothing is certain: every piece has circulated, changed hands, passed through places long forgotten.

Some call them evidence, others call them fabrications. I compile them, stack them, and sell them. Truth doesn’t matter. Only the legend you take with you… and the caps you leave behind.
